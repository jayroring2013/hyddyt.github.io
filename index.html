About This Project

Hi! I'm Jay Roring. I started this project back in 2013 as a simple tracking site for light novels. Over the years, it has evolved into a community-driven analytics platform.

The goal is to provide transparent, data-driven insights for the LN/Anime/Manga community. All data is collected from public APIs (AniList, MyAnimeList, NovelUpdates) and community voting.

Open Source Community Driven Free to Use Vietnamese & English
